# AutomateFlow — Company Overview

AutomateFlow helps small and medium-sized businesses automate repetitive operational work using practical AI workflows. Founded as a fictional product company for this portfolio, AutomateFlow focuses on lead automation, customer support automation, document processing, CRM automation, and custom AI integrations.

Mission: reduce manual busywork so teams can spend more time with customers.

Typical buyers: COO, Head of Operations, VP Sales Ops, Support Director, Founder/CEO at 11–250 employee companies.
