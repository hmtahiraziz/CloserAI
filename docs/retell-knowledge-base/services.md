# Services

1. **Lead automation** — capture, enrich, route, and follow up on inbound leads.
2. **Customer support automation** — triage tickets, suggest replies, escalate exceptions.
3. **Document processing** — extract structured data from invoices, forms, and PDFs.
4. **CRM automation** — keep CRM records current; trigger playbooks from deal stages.
5. **Custom AI integrations** — connect AutomateFlow to existing tools and internal APIs.

Packages map services into Starter, Growth, and Custom AI Operations offerings.
